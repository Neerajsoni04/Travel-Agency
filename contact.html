Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book a tour through our website by visiting the Tours page, selecting your desired package, and clicking the "Book Now" button. Alternatively, you can contact our booking team directly by phone or email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, bank transfers, and in some cases, cash payments at our office locations.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our standard cancellation policy offers a full refund for cancellations made 45 days or more before departure. Cancellations made 30-44 days before departure receive a 50% refund. Cancellations made less than 30 days before departure are non-refundable. We recommend purchasing travel insurance for added protection.

Do you offer customized tour packages?

Yes, we specialize in creating customized tour packages tailored to your preferences, interests, and budget. Contact our team to discuss your dream vacation, and we'll help make it a reality.
